WELCOME TO PALMERSTON NORTH SQUASHGYM

SquashGym Palmerston North is one of New Zealand’s premier sporting club’s, host to many New Zealand National Squash events over the years including, the 1971 World Men’s Championship, the 2006 World Junior Men’s Championships and more recently the World Deaf Championships and the World Womens Teams Championships in 2010.
